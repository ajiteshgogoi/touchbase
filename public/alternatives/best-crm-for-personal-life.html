Frequently Asked Questions

How is a personal CRM different from social media?

Unlike social media's passive connection model, a personal CRM actively helps you maintain meaningful relationships through structured contact management, reminder systems and relationship insights. It focuses on depth rather than breadth of connections.

What types of relationships can I manage with a personal CRM?

Personal CRMs are versatile for managing family relationships, friendships, extended family connections, mentorship relationships and even professional networking. You can customize categories and tracking methods for each relationship type.

Is my personal data safe in a CRM?

Modern personal CRMs like TouchBase prioritize data privacy with end-to-end encryption, secure cloud storage and strict data protection policies. Your personal relationship data stays private and under your control.
