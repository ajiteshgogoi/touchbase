Alternatives to TouchBase

While TouchBase offers a comprehensive solution for networking and professional relationship management, it's essential to consider other options. Here are a few alternatives:

  • Dex: A popular personal CRM with a focus on networking and professional relationships.
  • Clay: A beautifully designed CRM that integrates with various social media platforms.
  • Monica: An open-source personal CRM with a strong emphasis on privacy.
  • Cloze: A multi-purpose CRM that attempts to automate much of the relationship tracking process by integrating deeply with email and other communication channels
  • Google Contacts: A basic but widely used contact management tool.
  • Notion: A versatile workspace tool that can be customized into a personal CRM with templates (Free tier available, Plus from $10/month).
  • Airtable: A powerful database platform that can be configured as a personal CRM (Free tier available, Team from $20/month).

Each of these alternatives offers unique features and benefits. The best choice depends on your individual needs and preferences.
