Frequently Asked Questions

What happens when I reach the 15 contact limit on the Free plan?

On the Free plan, you can actively manage up to 15 contacts. While you won't be able to add new contacts beyond this limit, your existing contacts and their data remain in the database. You'll only see reminders and suggestions for your 15 most recently added or interacted-with contacts. To manage more contacts, you can upgrade to Premium.

Can I cancel my Premium subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your Premium subscription at any time through your account settings. Your Premium access will continue until the end of your current billing period, after which your account will revert to the Free plan.

How is my data protected?

We take data privacy seriously. Your data is stored securely and is never shared with third parties. You have full control over your information and can export or delete it at any time. Please see our Privacy Policy for more details.

Do you offer a trial for the Premium plan?

Yes! New users automatically receive a 14-day free trial of TouchBase Premium when they sign up. No credit card is required for the trial.
